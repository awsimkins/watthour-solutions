OUR STORY

From Innovative Electric Services to Watthour Solutions

Founded in 2011 by Anthony W. Simkins, Watthour Solutions (formerly Innovative Electric Services) has been a trusted partner for Midwest utilities for decades.

We specialize in optimizing utility infrastructure through precise metering audits, accurate field testing, and strategic billing corrections. Our work has helped co-ops and utilities recover millions of dollars in lost revenue.
